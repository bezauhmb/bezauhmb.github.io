What is Tibeb?

Tibeb General Trading PLC is a women-owned trade and investment advisory firm dedicated to empowering foreign and local businesses. Specializing in bridging gaps across the value chain, Tibeb provides tailored solutions for investors, producers, suppliers, traders, and logistics stakeholders. The name "Tibeb," meaning wise and win-win practices, reflects the company's commitment to fostering smarter, sustainable businesses. By offering market insights, facilitating trade missions, and promoting system development, Tibeb helps businesses navigate Ethiopia's dynamic market and thrive globally. With a focus on expertise, collaboration, and innovation, Tibeb is shaping the future of trade and investment for sustainable growth and lasting impact.
